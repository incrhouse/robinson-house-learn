Funding Your Program

allocated for this.

State workforce AI training programs qualify for multiple existing federal funding streams. We'll help you identify and align your proposal to the right one.

WIOA Title I

Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act

Adult, dislocated worker, and youth programs. AI Unlocked qualifies as occupational skills training under Title I funding streams.

OMB M-25-21

Federal AI Workforce Mandate

All federal agencies are now required to build AI-capable workforces and document progress. AI Ready is structured to meet this compliance requirement directly.

AI for Healthcare qualifies for clinical workforce training funding. Dr. Cleve's licensure and clinical background make this a direct fit for behavioral health workforce grants.
